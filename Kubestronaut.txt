The Kubestronaut program is an initiative by the Cloud Native Computing Foundation (CNCF) designed to recognize individuals who have demonstrated exceptional commitment and expertise in Kubernetes and cloud-native technologies. Launched in March 2024, the program honors those who have successfully obtained all five of CNCF’s Kubernetes-related certifications:

KCNA – Kubernetes and Cloud Native Associate

KCSA – Kubernetes and Cloud Security Associate

CKA – Certified Kubernetes Administrator

CKAD – Certified Kubernetes Application Developer

CKS – Certified Kubernetes Security Specialist
